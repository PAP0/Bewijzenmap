DAY IN THE LIFE



To start the day off, I wake up at around 7:00AM And have half an hour to get ready to head out to school. I always leave home at 7:35AM and travel to school with a train. Depending on the day, I'm at school for 5 or 6 hours. I travel back home the same way I travel to school. After coming home I grab something to eat and start working on school projects. Sometimes I finish them when I'm at school but when I don't, I finish them at home. When I'm finished working on the school projects, I like to either go to the gym, play some videogames or watch movies or series.
